• Mt. Cagua

    location_onBrgy. Magrafil, Gonzaga, Cagayan

  • The Best Adventure

    This 1, 133 meter active volcano is part of the Babuyan Island chain of volcanoes. Which is located at Magrafil, Gonzaga, Cagayan. The last eruption was in the year 1907. The LGU of Gonzaga, Tourism officer shared that it may take us 3-4 hour walk to reach the tip of the mountain. On our way, we will be mesmerize with different eye catching flora. We will also be passing by a two aesthetic water falls. He added that the journey is very tiring yet, when we will reach the peak, we'll witness its breath-taking beauty.
