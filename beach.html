Gonzaga's Most Visited Beach and Resort

Gonzaga has variety of resorts which offer several recreational activities. Staying with these resorts is a perfect time to spend family gatherings and special celebrations like birthday, anniversaries and the like. I always chose to celebrate such gatherings in the following resorts.



Tallag Beach and Rock Formationmore_vert location_onCabanbanan Norte, Gonzaga, Cagayan
Overviewclose

Tallag Beach not only gives you the perfect backdrop for photography. Its black sand glimmers with the setting of the sun. For the adventurer, the rock formations provide rock climbing activities. When the sea is calm, travelers can also explore the small caves and crevices in the area. During low tide, you can delight in secing marine animals like crabs, sea urchins, and shellfish on the shore

Gonzaga Riverview Resortmore_vert location_onPateng Gonzaga, Cagayan
Overviewclose

A charming river front resort and hotel with suits and dormitory type of rooms. Gonzaga Riverview Hotel and Resort offers you the opportunity to relax and enjoy a wonderful and outwitting riverside vacation in the beautiful town of Gonzaga. Located directly along the crystal clear flowing water of the Wangag River, claimed as “The Cleanest River in Region ll and a National Finalist.”

Balai BaiBai Beach Resortmore_vert location_onAmunitan Gonzaga, Cagayan
Overviewclose

This hotel is the best spot for you who desire a serene and peaceful getaway, far away from the crowds.

SASA Beach Resortmore_vert location_onIpil Gonzaga, Cagayan
Overviewclose

Nestled in the serene town of Gonzaga in the Philippines, Sasa Beach Resort boasts a picturesque shoreline that stretches invitingly with its fine, golden sand. This tranquil destination offers a myriad of activities to its visitors, including swimming, sunbathing, volleyball, and picnicking, ensuring a memorable experience for beach lovers of all ages. Sasa Beach Resort has few amenities, so plan your trip accordingly, although it does not have lifeguards on duty, bathroom access, allowances for dogs, or designated areas for camping and surfing.
