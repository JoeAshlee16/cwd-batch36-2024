The Caves of Gonzaga, located in the town of Gonzaga in Cagayan Province, Philippines, are a collection of remarkable limestone caves known for their unique formations and historical significance.

Cabanbanan Cave

location_onCabanbanan Norte, Gonzaga, Cagayan

Cabanbanan Norte is one of the lucky barangay of Gonzaga having a more accessible cave. This cave is 1.5 kilometers away from the National Road.

According to those who experienced seeing the cave, along their way, they passed by a vast rice field and its panoramic imagery as the opposite side of the road portrays a number of hills perfectly scattered

location_onPateng, Gonzaga, Cagayan
